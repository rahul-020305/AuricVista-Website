About AuricVista

AuricVista is a parent company that builds AI-powered businesses focused on solving real-world problems. Instead of working in just one industry, we have created an ecosystem of companies that improve student living, support farmers, reduce food waste, and promote renewable energy.

Every company under AuricVista shares one mission—to make everyday life smarter, more sustainable, and more impactful.
